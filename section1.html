Whales

Whales are the largest animals on Earth. They are known for their impressive size, intelligence, and complex behaviors.

There are two main types of whales: baleen whales and toothed whales. Baleen whales, such as the blue whale, have baleen plates for filtering food from water. Toothed whales, like the sperm whale, have teeth and often hunt individual prey.
